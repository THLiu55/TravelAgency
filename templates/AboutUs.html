Design

What we do?

As a family-owned shop that previously relied on walk-in customers, it needs to enter the online market with a new website that meets requirements for ease of use, quality communication, and bilingual support.

For the sake of a successful transformation from a basic walk-in travel agency to an online shop, our software engineering team has been hired to design and develop a website that meets user requirements and basic functionalities.

Customer Portal

Provides information on travel destinations, attractions, reviews, and accommodation, as well as booking and planning tools.

Staff Portal

Allows employees to organize, modify, prioritize, update, and keep track of bookings in the system.

Our Best Projects

To improve the customer using experiences, a smart chatting bot (AI customer service) is supported in our system. Reviews, machine learning based recommendations is also embedded.
